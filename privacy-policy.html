StreamSync Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

StreamSync is a Chrome extension that synchronizes video playback between users in real-time. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is used.

Data We Collect

StreamSync collects and processes the following data:

  • Email address — Used for account creation and authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • Display name — A username you choose, shown to other users in chat.
  • Authentication tokens — Used to verify your identity when signing in with Google.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not record what videos you watch.
  • We do not store chat messages on our servers — messages are relayed in real-time and not persisted.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Authentication: Your email and display name are used solely to identify you within the app and to other users in a room.
  • Settings: Your extension preferences (theme, accent color, notification settings) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and are never sent to any server.
  • Video Sync: Playback commands (play, pause, seek) are relayed through our server to synchronize video between room participants. No video content is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

StreamSync uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — For user login and account management. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) — For storing minimal account metadata (username change timestamps).

Data Security

All authentication is handled by Firebase, which uses industry-standard encryption. Communication between the extension and our server uses secure WebSocket connections.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained in Firebase for as long as your account exists. Chat messages are not stored. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

StreamSync is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
